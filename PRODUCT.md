# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Everyday English- and German-speaking mobile-first users who need to exchange a public contact, protect text or one file locally, and recover one private identity without an account. Desktop users expect a complete client-like workspace, not a stretched phone screen.

## Product Purpose

Chat NoControl is a static, backend-free browser app and PWA for local identity management and PPX encryption. Success means users can distinguish public from private material, complete critical flows without ambiguity, work offline after first load, and understand the product's limits without security hype.

## Brand Personality

Calm, plain, trustworthy. Dry bureaucratic satire belongs only in the name. Critical workflows stay direct, humane, and serious.

## Anti-references

- Crypto-hacker aesthetics, neon security theater, and fear-driven copy.
- Generic SaaS feature-card grids and decorative dashboards.
- Official Apple mimicry, fake Liquid Glass, or claims of Apple endorsement.
- Political messaging inside identity, encryption, recovery, or error flows.

## Design Principles

1. Public and private material must be impossible to confuse.
2. Familiar controls disappear into the task; novelty never competes with safety.
3. Every claim is narrow, testable, and honest about unaudited beta limits.
4. Mobile actions remain one-handed and reachable; desktop remains complete.
5. Local-first behavior is visible in copy, storage state, and network evidence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA target. Full keyboard and screen-reader operation, visible focus, 44x44 practical touch targets, 200% zoom/high reflow, reduced motion, non-color-dependent warnings, and complete EN/DE semantic parity are mandatory.
